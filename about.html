Our Story

Building champions and transforming lives in Miami since 2001

A Legacy of Champions

Founded in 2001, Family Taekwondo School has been Miami's home for traditional Korean Olympic martial arts for over two decades. What began as a passion for teaching has grown into a legacy of champions - both on the mat and in life.

From our humble beginnings, we've stayed true to our core belief: martial arts training should develop the whole person. We teach kicks, forms, and sparring, but more importantly, we instill discipline, confidence, respect, self-control, and teamwork in every student who walks through our doors.

Today, Family Taekwondo School serves students of all ages - from our Little Ninjas as young as 3 years old to adults seeking fitness, self-defense, and personal growth. We're proud to be a place where families train together and lifelong friendships are formed.

Competition Excellence

Family Taekwondo School has produced some of the nation's top fighters in every category: Junior (6-17 years old), Senior (17-32 years old), and Executive Senior (32 and up).

Training with FTS and representing the school in multiple competitions develops self-confidence, good sportsmanship, and team spirit. Our students regularly compete in AAU National Championships and other prestigious tournaments.
