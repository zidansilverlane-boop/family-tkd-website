Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our memberships

What's included in the free trial?
+
Your free trial includes a full class experience in your age-appropriate program. You'll participate alongside current students, learn basic techniques, and get a feel for our teaching style. No purchase or commitment required—just come ready to learn!
Do I need to buy a uniform right away?
+
No, you don't need a uniform for your first few classes. We recommend comfortable athletic clothing initially. Once you decide to continue, we offer affordable uniforms and payment plans are available for gear purchases.
Are there any registration or belt testing fees?
+
We keep additional fees minimal. Basic belt testing is included in monthly membership fees. Higher belt tests and specialty certifications may have nominal fees. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden costs.
Can I switch between programs?
+
Absolutely! As students grow and advance, they can transition between programs. Our Family and Competition packages include access to multiple programs. Individual plans can be upgraded at any time without penalty.
What's your cancellation policy?
+
We operate on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contracts. If you need to cancel, simply provide 30 days notice. We also offer membership holds for vacations or temporary circumstances.
Is the Special Needs program really free?
+
Yes, 100% free! Through the Taekwondo 1 Dream Foundation, we provide complimentary martial arts instruction for children with Down Syndrome and other special needs. There are no hidden fees or future charges. Contact us to enroll your child.
