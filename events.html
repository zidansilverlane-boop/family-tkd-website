January 15, 2025

Team FTS Brings Home 12 Medals from Regional Championship

Our competition team had an outstanding performance at the South Florida Regional Championship, earning 5 gold, 4 silver, and 3 bronze medals across multiple divisions. Congratulations to all our athletes who represented Family Taekwondo School with excellence and sportsmanship. Special recognition goes to Maria Santos (gold in Junior Forms) and Kevin Rodriguez (gold in Teen Sparring) who will advance to state finals.
