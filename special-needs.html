Joseph's Story

When Master Vicente Albuerne's son Joseph was born with Down Syndrome, he faced the same questions many parents of special needs children face: How can I help my child develop confidence? How can I help them improve their coordination and focus? How can I give them the same opportunities as other children?

The answer came through Taekwondo. When Joseph joined Family Taekwondo School at age 5, something remarkable happened. His coordination improved. His discipline strengthened. His concentration sharpened. Most importantly, his self-esteem soared.

Today, Joseph competes in local, national, and international championships—sometimes against children without any condition. His journey inspired Master Vicente to extend these life-changing benefits to every child, regardless of their abilities.

"Through our program, students experience improved security, reflexes, and discipline. They develop confidence in themselves and social skills that open doors despite their condition." — Master Vicente Albuerne

What We Teach

Our Special Needs Program follows the same traditional Taekwondo curriculum as our other programs, adapted to meet each student's unique abilities and learning pace.

  • ✓ Traditional Taekwondo Basics — Stances, blocks, kicks, and strikes
  • ✓ Forms (Taeguks) — Choreographed movements that build memory and coordination
  • ✓ Physical Training — Exercises to build strength, flexibility, and endurance
  • ✓ Hip Hop Dance — Fun movement classes to develop artistic abilities
  • ✓ Belt Progression — Same ranking system, celebrating every achievement
Taekwondo 1 Dream Foundation

A 501(c)(3) Nonprofit Organization

The Taekwondo 1 Dream Foundation was established to provide free martial arts instruction to children with Down Syndrome and other special needs. Through the generosity of our community and supporters, we're able to offer this life-changing program at no cost to families.

Our mission is simple: give every child, regardless of their abilities, the opportunity to discover their potential through the discipline and joy of Taekwondo.
