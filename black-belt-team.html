Competition Excellence

Black Belt Team

Elite competition training for dedicated martial artists pursuing excellence at the highest level

Compete. Conquer. Repeat.

The Black Belt Team at Family Taekwondo School is our elite competition program, designed for students who want to take their martial arts to the next level. Under the guidance of Master Vicente Albuerne, our competitors train with intensity, discipline, and purpose.

Whether you're preparing for your first local tournament or aiming for national and international championships, our program provides the advanced technical training, strategic conditioning, and mental preparation you need to succeed on the competition floor.

Our team members represent Family Taekwondo School at tournaments throughout Florida and across the country, consistently bringing home medals and making our school proud.

Training Disciplines

What We Train

Our competition program covers all major Taekwondo disciplines to develop well-rounded martial artists

🥊

Olympic Sparring

Full-contact competitive sparring following World Taekwondo rules. Speed, timing, and strategy training for tournament-ready fighters.

🤸

Forms (Poomsae)

Precision-focused training in traditional and freestyle forms. Every stance, block, and strike executed with power and perfection.

✊

Breaking

Power breaking and speed breaking techniques that demonstrate focus, strength, and technical mastery at competition level.

🛡

Self-Defense

Practical self-defense techniques rooted in traditional Taekwondo. Real-world application of martial arts skills and awareness.
